Drop-Off Service

Can’t make it during our regular hours? No problem! Use our convenient drop-off box.

How It Works:

  • Mark/Pin the areas on your clothes that need alteration.
  • Place your clothes in a bag.
  • Include a note with your contact information and alteration instructions inside the bag.
  • Drop the bag in our drop-off box.

We will pick up the drop-offs daily and get to work on your garments promptly.
